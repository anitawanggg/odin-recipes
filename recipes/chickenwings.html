Chicken Wings Recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken wings
  • Ginger and scallion
  • Spices
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Boil chicken wings to half cooked
  2. Put in ginger and scallion and sear wings
  3. Add soy sauces
  4. Submerge in boiling water and add spices
  5. Wait 20 minutes and serve
